The Weekly Cycle

How AVauction works

Every week follows the same structured rhythm — so you always know exactly when to browse, bid, and win.

👁
Monday
Preview
New lots go live. Browse the week's inventory, download spec sheets, and save your shortlist.
📋
Tuesday
Bidding Opens
Place opening bids. Set your max and let our proxy bidding handle the rest automatically.
📈
Wednesday
Active Bidding
Price discovery in full swing. Monitor your lots and adjust strategy in real time.
🔔
Thursday
Final Push
Outbid alerts go out. Last chance to review and raise before the Friday close.
🔨
Friday
Auction Closes
Bidding ends at 5 PM ET. Winners are notified immediately and invoiced within the hour.
🏷️

Buy It Now

Don't want to wait for Friday? Sellers can list gear at a fixed price — visible any day, available until sold.

🤖

Proxy Bidding

Set your maximum and walk away. The system bids on your behalf, raising only as needed — never over your limit.

📦

Logistics Coordination

We connect buyers with vetted freight partners who handle AV gear specifically — crated, insured, and trackable.

Built for Professionals

Trust is the product

Every part of AVauction is built around giving both sides of a deal complete confidence — before, during, and after the transaction.

🔒

Escrow Protection

Funds are held in escrow until the buyer confirms receipt and condition. No payment until you're satisfied — no exceptions.

✅

Verified Sellers

Every seller undergoes business verification before their first listing. We confirm entity, licensing, and transaction history — no anonymous accounts.

📊

Condition Grading

Standardized A–D grading with photo evidence requirements. You know exactly what "excellent condition" means on AVauction — because we define it.

💬

Pre-Auction Q&A

Ask sellers anything during the preview window. Questions and answers are public so the whole buyer pool benefits from the transparency.

⭐

Seller Reputation

Verified transaction history, response-rate tracking, and dispute outcomes are all visible on seller profiles. Reputation compounds over time.

🛡️

Dispute Resolution

Dedicated resolution team with a 48-hour response SLA. Disputes are mediated by AV industry professionals — not generalist support staff.
